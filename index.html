#Writing on the Web with Markdown *_This is a sample text for testing [Markdown]._ ##What is Markdown? Markdown is a writing format allowing writers, journalists, bloggers, and other wordsmiths to easily write meant to be *easy to read & write*. Writers normally do not want to bog down their posts, novels, and other measures of prose with HTML tags. They want the ease of writing naturally for a markedup enivronment. This is the beauty of Markdown. It's simplistic yet robust and is fairly easy to use, even for those who usually shun writing on the web.
