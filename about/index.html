UMAYAMU is a curation of cultural and artistic elegance by Aamaal Abdul-Malik.

Fueled by an affinity for beauty and culture, Aamaal serves as your guide to discovering beauty from Africa, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia. Weekly features on fashion, jewellery, accessories, and more are focused on revealing the people, cultures, and techniques behind some of the world's most beautiful adornments.

As an advocate of the arts and sciences, cultural preservation and exchange, and artistic expression, Aamaal firmly believes that art and culture are the cornerstones of meaningful experiences and social change.

In addition to UMAYAMU, Aamaal is a luxury strategist and consultant, helping organizations refine brand communications and retail spaces, strengthen customer relationships and cultural competency, and create engaging brand experiences. She is the Director of THESAURAI PR, the first and only U.S.-based PR & Events agency exclusively representing couture and contemporary ready-to-wear brands and designers from the Middle East & Africa. She is also the Contributing Fine Jewelry Editor at EAT LOVE SAVOR Magazine, where she writes about rare and stunning fine jewellery and historic and innovative timepieces.
